Hi, I'm

Jiakai Zhang

Assistant Researcher · Co-founder @ Cellverse

I'm an Assistant Researcher at the VRVC Lab, ShanghaiTech University, where I also received my Ph.D. under Prof. Jingyi Yu. My research focuses on AI for structural biology — making Cryo-EM reconstruction faster, more accurate, and accessible through foundation models.

Beyond the lab, I co-founded Cellverse (寰渺科技) to bring these tools to real-world drug discovery. I've also spent time at Meta Reality Labs working on neural rendering and AR/VR.
